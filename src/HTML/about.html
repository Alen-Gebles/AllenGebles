About Me.

My name is Allen Gebles, I'm a Front-End Developer and UI/UX Designer based in Serbia.

Journey started in 2018 after I enrolled in Information Technology HS, where I discovered my passion for coding and web development. Since then, I've immersed myself in mastering CSS, and JavaScript and React both in and out of the classroom.

I enjoy creating visually engaging websites that offer outstanding user experiences and combining it with my sense of design. When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually hiking, reading, annoying my cat, or going into the forest and foraging mushrooms. I like ants too.
